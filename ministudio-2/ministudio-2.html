WE SHOULD TAKE A MOMENT TO ACKNOWLEDGE THE LAND ON WHICH WE ARE GATHERED.

FOR THOUSANDS OF YEARS, THIS LAND HAS BEEN THE HOME OF PATWIN PEOPLE. TODAY, THERE ARE THREE FEDERALLY RECOGNIZED PATWIN TRIBES:

CACHIL DEHE BAND OF WINTUN INDIANS OF THE COLUSA INDIAN COMMUNITY, KLETSEL DEHE WINTUN NATION, AND YOCHA DEHE WINTUN NATION.

THE PATWIN PEPLE HAVE REMAINED COMMITTED TO THE STEWARDSHIP OF THIS LAND OVER MANY CNETURIES.

IT HAS BEEN CHERISHED AND PROTECTED, AS ELDERS HAVE INSTRUCTED THE YOUNG THROUGH GENERATIONS.

WE ARE HONORED AND GRATEFUL TO BE HERE TODAY ON THEIR TRADITIONAL LANDS.
